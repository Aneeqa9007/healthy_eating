Confetti Quinoa Salad
(Makes 8-9 cups)

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup uncooked quinoa
  2. 6 cups water
  3. 1 cup frozen corn
  4. 1 can (19 fl oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  5. 1 yellow pepper (seeds and ribs removed), diced
  6. 1/2 red onion, diced
  7. 4 Roma tomatoes, diced
  8. ½ English cucumber, diced
  9. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  10. 2-3 tablespoons parsley, minced
  11. ½ cup olive oil
  12. ½ cup white vinegar
  13. salt and pepper to taste

Procedure

1. Put the quinoa and water in pot and bring the water to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer 10-15 minutes until the quinoa is soft. Drain water off using colander. Fluff with a fork and let it cool in the refrigerator. 2. Measure out the frozen corn and add to the quinoa. 3. Drain the black beans. Rinse the black beans under water. Add to the quinoa and corn mixture. 4. Mince up garlic and parsley. 5. Measure out all the oil and vinegar. 6. Mix everything together. Stir in olive oil and vinegar and add salt and pepper to taste. 7. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving size: 1 cup
  • Calories: 186 kcal
  • Carbohydrates: 20 g
  • Protein: 8 g
  • Fat: 10 g
