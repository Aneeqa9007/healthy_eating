African Style Peanut Stew
(Makes 4 bowls)

Ingredients

  1. 2 tablespoons peanut oil
  2. 1 red onion, chopped
  3. 2 tablespoons chopped fresh ginger
  4. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  5. 450 grams chicken, cut into chunks (Optional)
  6. 1 tablespoon crushed red pepper, or to taste
  7. salt and ground black pepper to taste
  8. 5 cups chicken stock
  9. 5 cups chicken stock
  10. 2 cups chopped tomatoes
  11. 1 cup chunky peanut butter
  12. Â½ cup coriander, roughly chopped

Procedure

1. Heat peanut oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. 2. Cook and stir onion, ginger, and garlic in hot oil until onion is softened, about 5 minutes. 3. Add chicken; cook and stir until completely browned. Season with crushed red pepper, salt, and black pepper. Pour in chicken stock. 4. Stir in sweet potatoes; bring to a boil. 5. Reduce heat to low, cover the pot partially with a lid, and cook at a simmer for 15 minutes. 6. Stir in tomatoes, peanut butter, and coriander leaves. 7. Partially cover the pot and continue cooking, stirring occasionally, until greens are tender, about 20 more minutes. 8. Serve hot with rice.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 731 kcal
  • Carbohydrates: 45 g
  • Protein: 46 g
  • Fats: 44 g
