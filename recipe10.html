High Protein Strawberry Smoothie
(Makes 2 glasses)

Ingredients

  1. 1 ½ cup milk
  2. 1 ripe frozen banana
  3. 2 cups strawberries
  4. ½ cup Greek yogurt
  5. 2 tablespoons good quality protein powder

Procedure

1. Place all the ingredients in a high-speed blender, and blend for 60-120 seconds until you get a smooth and creamy texture. 2. Taste the smoothie, adding more milk if it is too thick or adding more sweetener if it’s not sweet enough for you. Pour into a cup or mason jar. Enjoy immediately, or store in fridge for up to 24 hours.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 309 kcal
  • Carbohydrates: 43 g
  • Protein: 21 g
  • Fat: 7 g
