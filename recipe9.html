Cottage cheese pancakes:
Makes 6-8 pancakes

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup cottage cheese
  2. 3 eggs
  3. 1 ¼ cup whole wheat flour
  4. Zest of 1 lemon
  5. 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. ½ cup milk
  7. 1 tbsp baking powder
  8. ½ cup blueberries or strawberries

Procedure

1. Blend all the ingredients in a blender. 2. Heat a nonstick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it with oil or butter. 3. Pour ¼ cup at a time. Cook for 2-3 minutes on one side, or until bubbles start to form on the surface. 4. Flip the pancakes and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through. 5. Serve with toppings of your choice like maple syrup, honey, strawberries.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 280 kcal
  • Carbohydrates: 30 g
  • Protein: 40 g
  • Fats: 30 g
