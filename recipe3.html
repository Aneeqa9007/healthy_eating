Lentil Brownies
(Makes 15 2-inch squares)

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup canned brown lentils or boiled red lentils
  2. 3 Tbsp water
  3. ½ cup olive oil
  4. ¾ cup cocoa powder
  5. 1½ cup granulated sugar
  6. ¾ tsp salt
  7. 1 tsp vanilla extract
  8. 3 large eggs
  9. 1 cup whole wheat flour
  10. 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Procedure

1. Preheat oven to 350°F or 180°C. 2. In a food processor, puree lentils with water until smooth. 3. Mix lentil puree with oil, cocoa powder, sugar, salt, and vanilla extract. 4. Whisk eggs into the mixture, one at a time until evenly mixed. 5. Fold in flour and chocolate chips. 6. Line an 8” x 8” pan with parchment paper. 7. Add batter to pan. 8. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until centre appears firm. 9. Let cool before slicing into 16 - 2” squares and serving.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving size: 2” square (85 g)
  • Calories: 287 kcal
  • Protein: 5 g
  • Carbohydrates: 41 g
  • Fibre: 3 g
