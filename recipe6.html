Beef Barley Soup
(Makes 9 bowls)

Ingredients

  1. 1 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  2. 0.45kg diced stewing beef, seasoned with 1 tsp salt and 1 tsp pepper
  3. ½ large, sweet onion, diced
  4. 1 carrot, diced
  5. 2 celery stalks, chopped
  6. 2 tsp minced garlic
  7. 1 sweet potato, peeled and diced
  8. 1 large tomato, chopped
  9. 7 cups low-sodium beef stock
  10. ¼ cup kale or spinach powder (optional)
  11. ½ tsp ground turmeric
  12. ½ tsp red chilli powder
  13. 1 Tbsp whole grain mustard
  14. 1 Tbsp marjoram or fresh oregano
  15. 1 Tbsp thyme
  16. 2 Tbsp chia seeds
  17. 2 Tbsp flax seeds
  18. 2 Tbsp hemp hearts
  19. 2 Tbsp barley
  20. 1 tsp salt
  21. ½ tsp pepper

Procedure

1. Heat olive oil in pot over medium-high heat. 2. Add beef and cook until browned. 3. Set browned beef aside. 4. Add onion, carrot, celery, and garlic to pot. 5. Cook until soft. 6. Add remaining ingredients, including browned beef. 7. Bring to a boil. 8. Simmer for 45–60 minutes. 9. Serve hot.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving size: 1 bowl (340 mL)
  • Calories: 248 kcal
  • Protein: 18 g
  • Carbohydrates: 27 g
  • Fat: 5 g
  • Fibre: 5 g
