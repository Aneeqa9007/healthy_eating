Apple Cider Muffins
(Makes 12 Muffins)

Ingredients

  1. ½ cup apple cider
  2. 6 Tbsp plain 2% Greek yogurt
  3. 1 egg
  4. 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. ¾ cup unsweetened applesauce
  6. 1 cup bran
  7. ½ cup brown sugar
  8. 1 cup whole wheat flour
  9. 1 Tbsp baking powder
  10. ½ tsp salt
  11. 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  12. 2 Tbsp wheat germ

Procedure

1. Preheat oven to 375°F or 190°
2. Grease standard muffin
3. Whisk together cider, yogurt, egg, vanilla extract, and applesauce.
4. Soak bran in cider mixture for 5 minutes
5. In a separate bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and wheat germ.
6. Add wet bran mixture to the dry ingredients. Mix until moistened.
7. Fill muffin cups 2/3 full of batter.
8. Bake for 25-30 minutes.
9. Serve warm or wrap and refrigerate.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving size: 1 muffin
  • Calories: 121 kcal
  • Protein: 3 g
  • Carbohydrates: 25 g
  • Total fat: 1 g
