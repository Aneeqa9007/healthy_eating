Chickpea and lentil rice bowl
(Makes 4 bowls)

Ingredients

  1. 1.8 cups chickpeas soaked overnight and boiled
  2. 1 tbsp olive oil
  3. 1 tsp red chilli powder
  4. 1/2 tsp turmeric
  5. 1/2 tsp salt
  6. 1/2 tsp pepper
  7. 1 tbsp olive oil
  8. 1 onion diced
  9. 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  10. 1/2 lime juiced
  11. 2 1/2 cup cooked lentils
  12. 1/2 tsp vegetable stock
  13. 1/4 cup water
  14. 1/4 cup green chutney
  15. 2 cups cooked rice (white or brown)
  16. 4 cups spinach or greens of choice
  17. 2 avocados diced (optional)
  18. 1 lemon
  19. Green onion & coriander chopped

Procedure

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Add olive oil, turmeric, salt, pepper to the chickpeas in the baking sheet and toss to combine. Place in the oven and roast for 20 minutes, flipping halfway. 2. Prepare green chutney (blend together mint leaves, coriander leaves, green chillies, garlic, olive oil, salt, pepper, lemon juice) and set aside. 3. While the chickpeas are roasting, prepare the lentils. Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the onion and ginger garlic paste to the pan and cook until soft (approx. 3-5 minutes). Add lime juice to the pan. 4. Add the cooked lentils, vegetable stock, water, green chutney to the pan and stir to combine. Keep pan on low heat until ready to serve. 5. To assemble, add equal parts of brown rice, spinach, lentils and chickpeas to each bowl. Top with avocados, lime wedges, green onion, coriander and green chutney. 6. Serve hot.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 626 kcal
  • Carbohydrates: 76 g
  • Protein: 28 g
  • Fat: 27 g
