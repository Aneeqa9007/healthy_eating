Mulligatawny Soup
(Makes 6 bowls)

Ingredients

  1. ¼ cup extra virgin olive oil
  2. 1 carrot, diced
  3. ½ onion, diced
  4. 2 tsp minced garlic
  5. 1 large tomato, diced
  6. 3 cups low-sodium vegetable broth
  7. 1¾ cup unsweetened coconut milk
  8. 1 cup dried red lentils
  9. ½ cup kale powder (optional)
  10. 2 Tbsp chia seeds
  11. 2 Tbsp flax seeds
  12. 2 Tbsp hemp hearts
  13. ½ tsp ground ginger
  14. ¼ tsp ground cardamom
  15. ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
  16. ¼ tsp ground paprika
  17. ¼ tsp ground cumin
  18. 1 Tbsp ground turmeric
  19. 1 Tbsp curry powder
  20. ¾ tsp salt
  21. 2 apples, peeled and small diced

Procedure

1. Heat olive oil in pot over medium heat. 2. Add carrot, onion, and garlic. 3. Cook until soft. 4. Add remaining ingredients, except apples. 5. Bring to a boil. 6. Simmer for 50 minutes with the lid on. 7. Add diced apples. 8. Simmer for 20 minutes, then serve hot.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving size: 1 bowl (340 mL)
  • Calories: 490 kcal
  • Protein: 15 g
  • Carbohydrates: 48 g
  • Fats: 13 g
